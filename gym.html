General Notes
📈 Add weight when you reach the top of the rep range across all sets (progressive overload).
⏱️ Compound lifts: 2–3 min rest. Isolation exercises: 60–90 sec rest.
🏃 HIIT on Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri mornings only. No HIIT on Wed, Sat, or Sun.
😴 Sunday is full rest — recovery is when adaptation actually happens.
🥩 Target 130–150 g protein per day. Estimated cost ₹120–160/day (Indian market).
